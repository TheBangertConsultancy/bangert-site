About the Founder

Thomas Bangert

Founder & Principal Consultant

With over 25 years shaping the digital strategies of the world's leading travel platforms, Thomas brings a rare combination of global brand experience and hands-on market knowledge to every engagement.

From leading EU operations at TripAdvisor to building programmatic advertising at Amadeus to scaling revenue at lastminute.com, Thomas has sat on every side of the travel marketing table. Today, he channels that experience into helping destinations, hotel groups, DMOs, and travel tech companies grow smarter and faster.

Based between Romania and the UK, Thomas also co-founded Ruuts.travel, Ruuts.pro, and iWander, ventures blending digital storytelling with responsible tourism innovation.
